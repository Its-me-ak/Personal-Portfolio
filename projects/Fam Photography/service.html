What we do ?

We are a photography studio specializing in wedding, HDR, landscape, macro, hi speed, jewelry, birthday and fashion photography, based in New Delhi. We love to turn ideas into beautiful things.

Since inception, we have delivered more than 9 million words to 490 clients from 23 countries around the world. Our customer retention rate is around 95%. This is why 80% of our work has been accepted the very first time and never returned to us for revision.

We do have our fair share of failures too. In the past five years, 32 pieces of content have been rejected by the clients. We have introspected and ensured that this number goes down in the next five years.
